# Mirror Sync Receipts Policy

This document outlines the governance policy for mirror sync receipts within the Black Hole PromptOps registry.

## Append-Only Receipts
To guarantee the integrity and immutability of mirrored artifacts, the registry relies on mirror sync receipts. These are treated as append-only artifacts.

## Verification
All valid sync receipts must be verified against approved official mirrors.

## Discrepancies
Any failures or discrepancies detected during the sync process must be flagged.
