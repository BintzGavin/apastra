# Content Takedown Policy

## Purpose
This policy outlines the procedure for requesting the removal of prompts or related artifacts from the public registry due to violations of our Acceptable Use Policy, intellectual property infringement, or other safety concerns.

## Reporting a Violation
To report a violation, users must submit an issue using the `moderation_report.yml` template in the repository. The report must include:
1. The exact canonical name or URI of the prompt package.
2. The specific rule from the Acceptable Use Policy that is being violated.
3. Supporting evidence (e.g., screenshots, output examples, or legal documentation).

## Takedown Process
1. **Initial Review**: The Governance Team will review the report within 48 hours of submission.
2. **Temporary Suspension**: If the report indicates a severe safety or legal risk, the package may be temporarily suspended pending investigation.
3. **Investigation**: The Governance Team will investigate the claim, potentially reaching out to the reporter and the package owner for clarification.
4. **Decision**: Based on the investigation, the Governance Team will either:
   - Issue a permanent takedown and append a rejection record to the registry.
   - Re-instate the package if no violation is found.
   - Request the owner to make specific modifications within a given timeframe.

## Transparency
All takedown decisions (excluding sensitive legal or safety details) will be documented in the registry's public moderation log to maintain transparency.
