# Takedown Policy

This document outlines the procedure for submitting and processing content takedown requests.

## Request Submission
Requests must include:
- A link to the specific prompt package.
- The reason for the takedown request (e.g., copyright violation, violation of acceptable use policy).
- Contact information of the requester.

## Review Process
1. A Governance Admin will review the request within 3 business days.
2. If the request is valid, the content will be temporarily hidden from the public registry while an investigation takes place.
3. The content owner will be notified and given a chance to appeal.

## Final Decision
A final decision will be made within 14 days. If the takedown is permanent, the package will be completely removed from the registry, and a tombstone record will be left in its place.
