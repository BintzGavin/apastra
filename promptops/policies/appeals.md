# Appeals Policy

This document outlines the procedure for appealing a moderation decision (e.g., a takedown or rejection).

## Appeal Submission
The content owner must submit an appeal within 7 days of the moderation decision. The appeal must include:
- The ID of the original moderation decision.
- A detailed explanation of why the decision should be overturned.
- Any supporting evidence.

## Review Process
1. A different Governance Admin (who was not involved in the original decision) will review the appeal.
2. The review will be completed within 5 business days.

## Final Decision
The decision of the appeal review is final.
